Cosmic Inferno, invocation to thee!
Vengeance that calls me forth from the dawning of eternity!
Smite thy deceiver cast into darkness!
Burning, writhing beneath the serpent's kiss.
An avenger is born - birthed from the ashes of retribution!
Treacherous, scornful existence.
Shedding the waterless tears that bound him in darkness.
The sunrise that came whispered your name,
Through the decaying of time, the afflication of loss -
your radiance exudes the bleakest of sorrows.
Life dissolves.
