I carry on catenated by infinite obscurity.
Infringing upon the light, sculpting my somber dominion.
Shrouded landscapes scourged by foolish mortals
From the bleakness of this domain, enthroned I shall be!
Centuries decompose, forever buried and forgotten in time.
Man falls breathless, dissolving into the weeping macrocosm.
But I persist to necessitate abysmal rest upon iniquitous men,
to cloak abstruse and ancient powers,
trailed by darkness - dispelling day!
Forever perpetuating my name onto the lips of the horizon!

(A new and waning moon, the beneficiant powers created to count the years of men)
