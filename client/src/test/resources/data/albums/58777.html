There are no reviews for My Death for You on a Night of Cursed Full Moon / Pronimo yet. You can write one.

Includes lyric sheet, 2 posters and 2 stickers (one for each band).

Also available on cassette, limited to 200 copies.

- Bewitched credits:
All music by Bewitched, except track 5 (outro) written by John Hotsby (1410-1487)
Doucaines on track 5 (outro) by Medieval Wind Ensemble
Produced by Aldo Doomicus, Cecilia, Rodrigo & Christian
All lyrics by Aldo Doomicus

- F credits:
All the musical creations included are totally composed, played, produced and recorded by F
Lyrics from Finish to English translated by Mikko Mattila
Layout designs by the F realization Markku Malkki

Recording information:

- Bewitched tracks (1 to 5) was recorded and mixed in 4 hours at Bellavista Studios.

- F track 6 was recorded at Ingemarg during 1/2 /93 to 5 /12 /93 & 28/2/93 to 5/3/93.

Identifiers:

- Barcode: None

- Matrix Code (Side B): F-UNCUNT 10-V-93 WAR 002
