Footage from D's 10th anniversary live concert held at Shibuya Koukaidou on April 12, 2013. The second disc contains footage from D's ten days of live shows at Takadanobaba Area leading up to the final concert. This set was released exclusively to members of Rosen Kranz, D's official Japanese fanclub.
