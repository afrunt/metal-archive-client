D are one of the leading bands in the Visual Kei movement in Japan.

The band entered a hiatus at the end of 2014 while Asagi battled temporomandibular joint disease. The group completed their tour and released "Kingdom" before the break. Their hiatus ended in mid 2015.

God Child Records are D's own label under which most of their early discography was released. In 2013 they changed from Avex Trax to FlyingStar Records; a subdivision of Victor, and in 2015 they were transferred to CJ Victor Entertainment.
